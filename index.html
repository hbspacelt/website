About HB Space TL

HB Space TL is a Malawian technology and robotics company focused on designing intelligent hardware and digital solutions that solve real-world challenges. We combine innovation, engineering, and skills development to empower communities and industries.

Based in Zomba, Malawi • Driven by Innovation • Built for Impact

Our Mission

To design and deliver impactful, affordable, and innovative technological solutions that improve lives, promote accessibility, and support sustainable development.

Our Values

Innovation – Creating forward-thinking solutions
Impact – Technology that makes a real difference
Integrity – Ethical and transparent operations

Quality – Reliable and durable systems
